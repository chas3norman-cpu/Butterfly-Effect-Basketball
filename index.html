About Butterfly Effect Basketball

My name is Charles Norman, and after years of training athletes and refereeing high-level basketball, I have seen a common problem: talented players often struggle to perform in games the same way they perform in workouts. At Butterfly Effect Basketball, I help athletes make small adjustments to the way they train and mentally approach the game so their work translates into in-game performance.

What I Train

Training is designed to help athletes build skill, control, confidence, and better decisions under pressure.

Ball Handling

Ball handling under pressure with better control, timing, and reaction.

Finishing

Game-speed finishing against realistic pressure and angles.

Shooting

Shooting mechanics, shot creation, and control when speed increases.

Footwork

Footwork and balance for better movement, control, and efficiency.

Decision-Making

Reaction training and faster reads in live situations.

Confidence

Confidence and composure training so skills show up in games.
